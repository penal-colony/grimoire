# Grimoire Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-06-21**

## The short version

Grimoire has no server. No database. No analytics. No tracking.

Your LLM API key never leaves your browser. Your conversations are not read or stored by Grimoire. Your lorebooks live on your device.

That's it.

## What does Grimoire collect?

Nothing. Zero. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage stats.

## Where is my data stored?

All lorebook content and settings are stored locally in your browser using its built-in storage. This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

## What about my API key?

Grimoire never reads, stores, or transmits your API key (OpenAI, OpenRouter, etc.). It modifies the chat request after JanitorAI has already constructed it, so your key is never exposed to the extension.

## What does Grimoire interact with?

- **JanitorAI pages** - Grimoire runs on `janitorai.com` to add lorebook content to your chats
- **The chat request** - When you send a message, Grimoire adds relevant lorebook entries to it before it reaches the AI

That's all. Grimoire does not interact with any other website or service.

## Permissions explained

| Permission | What it means |
|---|---|
| Side panel | Shows the Grimoire UI where you manage lorebooks |
| Storage | Saves your lorebooks and settings on your device |
| Tabs | Lets Grimoire open the side panel when you click its icon |
| Access to janitorai.com | Needed so Grimoire can work on your chats |

## How do I delete my data?

Remove the extension, or use your browser's extension data clearing option. All Grimoire data is removed instantly.

## Will this change?

The extension will never gain data collection or tracking features. If a future version adds any kind of network feature, it will be optional and clearly explained upfront.
